The first knows historical mention of the city comes from the early 12th-century, when thw chronicler Gallus Anonymus ranked it together with Krakow and Wroclaw as one of the main cities of Poland.THe testament (ca 1115-1118) of Boleslaw III Wrymouth,in which he devided Poland amoung his sons,designated Sandomierz as the capital of one of the resulting principalities, the Duchy of Sandomierz.
